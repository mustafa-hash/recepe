Palau is Kenyan food which originated from coastal Kenya
Palau can either prepared beef and rice or chicken and rice or
just rice alone.

Ingredients

250g beef
400g pishori rice,washed and
soaked for 15 minutes
2 onions chopped
1/2tsp tumeric powder
1/2 cumin powder
vegetable cooking oil
3 tomatoes
whole spaces
1/2 tsp whole pepper
2 cinnamon sticks

Steps



1. Cut the meat into pieces and wash,
then boil with some 1 tsp of salt
tsp of black pepper
1 grated tomato and 1 grated onion.
 2. When the meat is tender,drain it and set aside.
keep the soup cooking rice.
3. Heat the sufuriaon a medium heat and add the oil
. then add whole spices that is cumin and cinnamon.
Once they splutter in the oil. add chopped onions.
4. Fry them until translucent while stirring them with a spoon
Then add tumeric and coriander powder
5. Now you can add boiled meat stir together for 2 minutes.
Then add soup amnd some salt
6. When the soup start boiling, drain the rice
and add rice to the soup
Let it cook high heat for 4 minutes
then reduce to medium and keep cooking
7. Make sure you stir every now amd then
to make sure all rice is cooke.
switch off the heat and serve with kachumbari
